How it works

  1. Pick a case from the dossier. Each adventure is a .json file compatible with version 1 of the SHIC schema.
  2. Open the companion at sherlock.justplaybo.it; load the adventure from the menu — the app fetches it directly from this archive.
  3. Investigate. Roam London, gather circled letters, and — when you are ready — compare your solution with Holmes's.
